# SoMerch

**Merch that moves with your team.**

SoMerch is a software-powered corporate merch platform and service partner for distributed companies across Europe.

We help teams plan, produce, store, and ship branded merch from one place — combining curated products, in-house production, warehousing, inventory visibility, approvals, and EU-wide fulfillment.

---

## What SoMerch does

SoMerch brings corporate merch operations into one managed workflow:

- product curation and sourcing
- brand-consistent mockups
- in-house production and quality control
- kitting and pack-ins
- warehousing and inventory visibility
- multi-address and multi-country shipping
- approval workflows
- order and shipment tracking
- managed fulfillment for distributed teams

Our focus is not to offer thousands of generic products. We help companies choose practical, durable, brand-safe merch that works in real business use cases.

---

## Built for distributed teams

SoMerch is designed for companies with remote employees, multiple offices, onboarding waves, event kits, and cross-border shipping needs.

Common use cases include:

- employee onboarding packs
- remote team gifting
- event and conference kits
- office restocking
- partner and customer gifts
- internal brand campaigns
- managed inventory programs

## Product direction

SoMerch is building the operational layer for modern corporate merch.

Current and planned focus areas include:

- curated corporate merch catalog
- quote and order tracking
- stock visibility
- multi-address order uploads
- employee redeem links
- office and team portals
- approval workflows
- shipment status visibility
- HR and procurement integrations
- AI-assisted merch recommendations
- EU-focused logistics and compliance workflows

---

## Why this matters

Corporate merch is no longer just a bulk order for one office.

Modern teams are distributed across cities, countries, and time zones. Operations, HR, procurement, marketing, and office teams need a clearer way to manage branded items without vendor chaos, spreadsheet overload, or manual shipment coordination.

SoMerch combines service, software, production, warehousing, and fulfillment into one managed system.
